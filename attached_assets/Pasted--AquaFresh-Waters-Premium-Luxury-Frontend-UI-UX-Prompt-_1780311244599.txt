# AquaFresh Waters – Premium Luxury Frontend UI/UX Prompt

Design and build an ultra-premium, award-winning frontend experience for a drinking water brand called **AquaFresh Waters**.

The website should feel like a blend of:

* Apple Product Launch Website
* Tesla Website
* Liquid Death Premium Branding
* Bisleri Vedica Luxury Experience
* Nike Storytelling Website
* Awwwards-winning modern web experiences

The goal is to make visitors instantly feel:

**Pure • Premium • Luxury • Trustworthy • Refreshing • Modern**

---

# Design Language

Create a highly immersive, cinematic experience.

Color Palette:

* Crystal White
* Aqua Blue
* Deep Ocean Blue
* Ice Blue
* Silver Accents

Visual Style:

* Glassmorphism
* Neumorphism
* Luxury Product Showcase
* Smooth Gradients
* Premium Typography
* Floating Elements
* Soft Reflections
* Liquid-Inspired Animations

The entire website should feel fluid, elegant and expensive.

# Final Goal

The website should not look like a normal water company website.

It should feel like a luxury product launch experience where users are emotionally convinced that AquaFresh is a premium water brand before they even see the pricing.

The experience should be memorable, immersive, cinematic, and worthy of an Awwwards Website of the Day nomination.
